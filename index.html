Circuit Locations

  • Sakhir, Bahrain

    The Bahrain World Trade Center in Manama is a twin tower complex, the first skyscrapers in the world to integrate wind turbines into their design. Developed by Bahrain World Trade Center and designed by Atkins.

  • Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

    The Jeddah Tower, once completed, is set to be the world's tallest building, surpassing the Burj Khalifa in Dubai. Developed by Jeddah Economic Company and designed by Adrian Smith.

  • Melbourne, Australia

    The Eureka Tower is one of the tallest residential buildings in the world, offering panoramic views of the city. Developed by Grocon and designed by Fender Katsalidis Architects.

  • Suzuka, Japan

    While Suzuka is more known for its racing circuit, nearby Nagoya boasts the Mode Gakuen Spiral Towers, a unique architectural marvel. Developed by Mode Gakuen and designed by Nikken Sekkei.

  • Shanghai, China

    The Shanghai Tower is China's tallest building and the world's second-tallest, with a unique twisted design and multi-use spaces. Developed by Shanghai Tower Construction and Development and designed by Gensler.

  • Miami, USA

    The Porsche Design Tower in Miami features a patented car elevator system, allowing residents to park their vehicles right outside their units in the sky. Developed by Dezer Development and designed by Sieger Suarez Architects.

  • Imola, Italy

    While Imola doesn't have skyscrapers, nearby Bologna has the Unipol Tower, the tallest building in the region. Developed by Unipol Group and designed by Open Project.

  • Monaco, Monaco

    The Odeon Tower in Monaco is one of the most expensive apartment complexes in the world, with a penthouse that boasts its own private slide. Developed by Groupe Marzocco and designed by Alexandre Giraldi.

  • Montreal, Canada

    The Tour des Canadiens is a notable skyscraper in Montreal, linked directly to the Bell Centre, home of the Montreal Canadiens. Developed by Canderel and designed by Cardinal Hardy.

  • Barcelona, Spain

    The Torre Glòries, formerly known as Torre Agbar, is an iconic skyscraper in Barcelona, known for its bullet-shaped design. Developed by Grupo Agbar and designed by Jean Nouvel.

  • Spielberg, Austria

    While Spielberg itself is small, nearby Graz has the Kunsthaus, a unique and modern art museum that stands out in the city's skyline. Developed by the city of Graz and designed by Peter Cook and Colin Fournier.

  • Silverstone, UK

    The Shard in London is the tallest building in the UK and offers a mix of commercial, residential, and observation spaces. Developed by Sellar Property Group and designed by Renzo Piano.

  • Budapest, Hungary

    The MOL Tower, once completed, will be Budapest's tallest building and serve as the headquarters for the MOL Group. Developed by MOL Group and designed by Foster + Partners.

  • Spa, Belgium

    While Spa doesn't have significant skyscrapers, nearby Liège has the Finance Tower Liège, a modern office building. Developed by Breevast and designed by Jaspers-Eyers Architects.

  • Zandvoort, Netherlands

    The nearby city of Rotterdam is known for its modern architecture, with De Rotterdam being one of its most iconic multi-use skyscrapers. Developed by De Rotterdam CV and designed by OMA.

  • Monza, Italy

    Milan, close to Monza, boasts the Bosco Verticale, or "Vertical Forest", residential towers known for their greenery-covered facades. Developed by Hines Italia and designed by Stefano Boeri.

  • Baku, Azerbaijan

    The Flame Towers in Baku are a trio of skyscrapers, including office, residential, and hotel spaces. Developed by Azinko Development MMC and designed by HOK.

  • Singapore, Singapore

    The Marina Bay Sands is an iconic integrated resort in Singapore, known for its unique ship-like structure atop three towers. Developed by Las Vegas Sands and designed by Moshe Safdie.

  • Austin, USA

    The Independent, also known as the "Jenga Tower", is the tallest residential building in Austin. Developed by Constructive Ventures and Aspen Heights and designed by Rhode Partners.

  • Mexico City, Mexico

    The Torre Reforma is Mexico City's tallest building and is known for its unique triangular design. Developed by LBR&A Arquitectos and designed by Benjamín Romano.

  • Sao Paulo, Brazil

    The Mirante do Vale is São Paulo's tallest building, primarily used for commercial purposes. Developed by Construtora Zarzur Kogan and designed by Waldomiro Zarzur and Aron Kogan.

  • Las Vegas, USA

    The Fontainebleau Resort Las Vegas, though currently unfinished, is set to be one of the tallest buildings in Las Vegas once completed. Developed by Fontainebleau Resorts and designed by Carlos Zapata Studio.

  • Losail, Qatar

    While Losail is known for its racing circuit, nearby Doha boasts the Burj Qatar, an iconic skyscraper with a unique cylindrical design. Developed by Hadi Simaan and designed by Jean Nouvel.

  • Yas Marina, UAE

    The nearby city of Abu Dhabi has the Etihad Towers, a complex of five skyscrapers. Developed by Sheikh Suroor Projects Department and designed by DBI Design.
